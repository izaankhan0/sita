Our Team

At our restaurant, we believe that great food begins with a great team. Our talented chefs bring passion, creativity, and dedication to every dish they prepare, ensuring that each plate is a reflection of their love for the culinary arts. From crafting bold, innovative flavors to perfecting timeless classics, our kitchen is a place where tradition meets creativity, and quality is at the heart of everything we do.
We work together with a shared goal: to create a dining experience that feels warm, inviting, and truly special. Whether it’s a carefully prepared entrée, a delectable dessert, or the perfect char on the grill, every bite tells a story of care and craftsmanship. Welcome to our table—we’re thrilled to share our passion for food with you.

Alessandro Raffaele

Culinary Maestro

With over 15 years of experience in the culinary world, Chef Alessandro Raffaele brings a passion for creating innovative, yet comforting dishes. Having trained in some of Italy’s finest kitchens, he combines traditional techniques with a modern flair to craft memorable dining experiences. Alessandro’s dedication to quality ingredients and attention to detail shines through in every plate, ensuring that each dish is a celebration of flavor and artistry. As the head of our kitchen, his leadership and creativity are the driving force behind our restaurant’s success.

Bhushan Sawant

Executive Chef

Chef Bhushan Sawant is a culinary visionary with a deep-rooted love for fusion cuisine. Drawing inspiration from his diverse training and travels, Bhushan has honed a unique style that blends classic techniques with bold, international flavors. With over a decade of experience in leading high-end kitchens, his expertise and commitment to excellence elevate every dining experience. As our Executive Chef, Bhushan oversees all aspects of our kitchen operations, ensuring that each dish is crafted with precision and passion, delivering an exceptional taste of innovation in every bite.

Simon Braithwait

Sous Chef

With a keen eye for detail and a love for culinary craftsmanship, Chef Simon Braithwait brings a wealth of experience and creativity to our kitchen. After years of working alongside top chefs across Europe, Simon has developed a refined palate and an expertise in elevating classic dishes with contemporary twists. As Sous Chef, Simon plays a pivotal role in executing the menu and mentoring our kitchen team, ensuring every dish meets the highest standards of quality and presentation. His passion for fresh, seasonal ingredients and his dedication to excellence are key to our kitchen's success.

So-yeon

Senior Chef

Chef So-yeon brings a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation to our kitchen. With a background rooted in her Korean heritage, she expertly fuses authentic flavors with modern techniques, creating dishes that are both comforting and exciting. Having worked in renowned kitchens around the world, So-yeon has refined her skills and developed a unique culinary voice that shines through in every plate. As Senior Chef, she is instrumental in menu development, overseeing kitchen operations, and ensuring the highest standards of quality and creativity. Her passion for food and dedication to excellence make her an invaluable part of our team.

Xavier Brooks

Grill Chef

Chef Xavier Brooks is a master of the grill, with a talent for bringing out the best in every cut of meat and vegetable. With years of experience in barbecue and open-fire cooking, he combines traditional grilling techniques with a deep understanding of flavor profiles to create dishes that are both smoky and savory. Xavier’s precision and passion for fire-cooked cuisine make him a standout in the kitchen. As Grill Chef, he ensures that every dish served from the grill is perfectly charred and bursting with flavor, providing our guests with a truly exceptional dining experience.
